        About 841G

        This site is currently under construction

        841G was originally founded by a team of sales people who worked together at a Best Buy who shared a common interest of getting together at regular times to play video games with each other over the internet. Over time however 841G has evolved to something more than just a couple coworkers blowing off steam after work to a community of many members who all share the same thing in common: a love for technology and video games. 841G is the best ongoing virtual LAN party where the fun and comradery never ends.
